Abilities & Tools

Abilities are superpowers you can give your sidekick. Each one connects your AI to a real-world service, letting it take action — not just talk.

On this page

How Abilities Work

When a user asks your sidekick something that requires an ability, the sidekick automatically decides which tool to use. You don't need to configure triggers or workflows — the AI understands when to use each ability based on the conversation.

For example, if a user says "Create a blog post about AI trends" and you have Content Catalyst enabled, your sidekick will recognize the intent and use that ability. If the user asks "What's the chance of a rate cut?" and Prediction Markets is enabled, it will pull live market data.

Tip: You can enable or disable abilities at any time from the Abilities page or from within the sidekick wizard. Only enable the abilities your sidekick actually needs — fewer abilities means faster, more focused responses.
Abilities — Available Tools

Content Catalyst

Content Catalyst
Research-backed article generation

Content Catalyst generates two high-quality, research-backed article variations from a single topic or source. It uses a multi-phase pipeline that mirrors a professional editorial workflow:

  1. Research — Gathers relevant sources from your knowledge base, the web (via Perplexity), and the conversation context
  2. Architecture — Designs the article structure and creates two different angles on the topic
  3. Drafting — Writes two full article variations with distinct perspectives
  4. Fact-checking — Verifies accuracy and validates sources
  5. Polishing — Refines prose, enforces word count, and ensures consistent tone

Accepted Input Types

  • Text — Describe a topic, paste notes, or provide a rough outline
  • Web URL — Provide a link and the tool will scrape and use it as source material
  • Audio (MP3) — Upload a podcast or recording to transcribe and turn into articles

What users will see

When a user asks your sidekick to write an article, the sidekick will process the request through all five phases and deliver two complete article variations. Users can choose the version they prefer or blend elements from both.

Content Catalyst works best when your sidekick also has access to your Knowledge Base. The research phase will incorporate your uploaded documents for more relevant, on-brand content.

Image Catalyst

Image Catalyst
AI-powered image generation

Image Catalyst lets your sidekick generate images on demand. It supports two modes optimized for different use cases:

Thumbnail / Promotional
Polished marketing images using GPT Image. Ideal for thumbnails, banners, logos, and promotional graphics. Supports reference images for style guidance.
General / Creative
Creative and artistic imagery using FLUX. Great for concept art, illustrations, and general creative visuals. Supports seed images for guided generation.

What users will see

When a user asks your sidekick to create an image, a widget opens in the chat where they can choose the mode, refine the prompt, and optionally upload a reference image. The generated image appears directly in the conversation.

Prediction Market Insights

Prediction Market Insights
Real-time probability data from Polymarket

This ability connects your sidekick to Polymarket, the world's largest prediction market. When users ask about the likelihood of future events, your sidekick returns real-time probability data backed by actual trading volume — not speculation.

What it covers

  • Elections and politics
  • Economic events (rate cuts, recessions, GDP)
  • Technology and AI milestones
  • Sports and entertainment
  • World events and geopolitics

What users will see

When a user asks something like "What's the probability of a government shutdown?", the sidekick searches Polymarket and returns the top matching markets with their current probabilities, trading volume, and resolution dates. Higher trading volume indicates stronger consensus among traders.

Your sidekick will automatically note that prediction market data reflects the wisdom of traders with real money at stake, but is not a guarantee of outcomes.

Web Search

Web Search
Real-time web information with citations

Web Search lets your sidekick query the internet in real-time to answer questions about current events, recent data, or anything not covered by your knowledge base. Powered by Perplexity AI, it returns comprehensive answers with source citations.

What users will see

When a user asks a question that requires current information, the sidekick searches the web, synthesizes the results, and presents the answer with inline citations. This is especially useful for questions about recent news, prices, schedules, or anything that changes frequently.

Lingua

Lingua
Audio transcription and multilingual subtitles

Lingua transcribes audio files and translates subtitles into 12+ languages with professional formatting. It's ideal for podcasts, webinars, interviews, and any audio content that needs to reach a multilingual audience.

Supported Languages

Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, Hindi, and more.

Output Formats

  • SRT — Standard subtitle format for video editors
  • WebVTT — Web-native subtitle format for HTML5 video
  • Plain Text — Full transcript without timing codes

What users will see

Users provide an audio file or URL, and the sidekick returns downloadable subtitle files in each requested language, along with the full text transcript and a confidence score.

Asana Task Manager

Asana Task Manager
Project and task management via voice or text

Connect your sidekick to Asana to manage projects and tasks through natural conversation. Your sidekick can create, update, complete, and list tasks — all hands-free.

What your sidekick can do

  • List tasks in a project (filtered by status)
  • Create new tasks with due dates and descriptions
  • Update or rename existing tasks
  • Mark tasks as complete
  • Create and manage subtasks
  • Assign tasks to team members

Setup

Asana requires an OAuth connection. Go to Abilities, find Asana Task Manager, and click Connect to authorize your Asana workspace. Once connected, configure which project(s) your sidekick should manage.

Important: The Asana connection uses OAuth, so your sidekick will only have access to the workspaces and projects you explicitly authorize.

HelpScout Connect

HelpScout Connect
Customer support ticket management

Connect your sidekick to HelpScout to manage support tickets through conversation. Your sidekick can read, respond to, and manage customer support conversations directly.

What your sidekick can do

  • List support tickets (filtered by active, pending, or closed)
  • View full ticket details and conversation history
  • Reply to customers with messages
  • Add internal notes visible only to your team
  • Close or reopen tickets
  • Assign tickets to specific team members
  • Create new support tickets

Setup

HelpScout requires an OAuth connection. Go to Abilities, find HelpScout Connect, and click Connect to authorize your HelpScout account. Configure which mailbox(es) your sidekick should monitor.

Enabling & Configuring Abilities

You can manage abilities from two places:

1

During Sidekick Creation

Step 4 of the Creation Wizard lets you toggle abilities on or off. The wizard pre-selects commonly used abilities for you.

2

From the Abilities Page

Navigate to Abilities in the admin panel to see all available tools. From here you can enable, disable, and configure each ability — including connecting OAuth accounts for Asana and HelpScout.

Pro Tip: Some abilities like Content Catalyst and Lingua work best when paired with a Knowledge Base. The more context your sidekick has, the better the output.

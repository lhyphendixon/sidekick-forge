Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade from Adventurer to Champion at any time. Your data will be seamlessly migrated to your own dedicated infrastructure, and you'll gain immediate access to all Champion features.

What's the difference between shared and dedicated infrastructure?

Adventurer tier runs on our shared cloud infrastructure, which is perfect for getting started. Champion and Paragon tiers get their own dedicated database and resources, providing better performance, data isolation, and the ability to scale without limits.

What is a "Sovereign Stack" in Paragon?

Paragon clients get complete infrastructure sovereignty - including the option for on-premise deployment, custom security configurations, and full control over where your data lives. Perfect for enterprises with strict compliance requirements.

Do you offer custom AI model training?

Custom model fine-tuning is available exclusively for Paragon clients. We work with you to train AI models specifically on your domain, voice, and brand to create truly unique sidekicks that sound and think like your team.
